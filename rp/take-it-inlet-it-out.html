Take It In/Let It Out

The Spark Saga · Zasia Sol/Sunni
October 30, 2025 – November 09, 2025
Zasia

The parcel hadn't been large, but still larger than its recipient had expected. The card attached indicated that it came from her elder sister, who sent her love along with a piece from her wardrobe that she recalled was admired. Untying the string and peeling aside the plain paper, any hopes she had for what she might find there lost the wings to fly and fell crashing back on terra firma. Zašia had hoped to find blue silk, but instead met with its polar opposite.

"I said I liked it on you," she muttered, as if her sister could hear the response. With a defeated sigh, she unpackaged the dress and put it on a hanger and set that on a hook on her door.

Can't wait to see you in this! Good luck on Sponsorship Day, Zša-Zša.

\- Mal

Amalia got her eye for what looked good on Zašia from their great uncle, she was certain now.

Resigned, she retrieved her sewing kit — her serious one, not the small one she used to repair buttons or loose seams. This was going to take effort that she would rather spend on her studies, but if her sister sent her a dress, then she had better be prepared to wear it. Was she coming for Sponsorship Day? Who the hell knew? Mal enjoyed the freedom to come and go with the wind, something her little sister envied.

The elder two Silverspan siblings were of marriageable age before scandal rocked their family, when their connections were more solid and their futures still shined brightly. They'd married who they chose, for love and companionship, and had their happiness. Rainier and Zašia were not so fortunate. It was up to them to forge new connections with more powerful families and help polish away the tarnish on their name.

It would be possible to do this alone, but it would be easier and go faster with a little bit of help. Quietly, Zašia tapped on her dormmate's door.

"Sol?" Her voice was equally soft, hoping to avoid disturbing him if he was resting between classes. "Do you have a moment?"
