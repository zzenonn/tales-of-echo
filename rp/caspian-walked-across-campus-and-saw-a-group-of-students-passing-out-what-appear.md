### &#x270D; Caspian
<sub>Feb 26, 2026 &middot; 07:54 PM UTC</sub>

Caspian walked across campus and saw a group of students passing out what appeared to be some sort of broadsheet to other students. 

He approached and they happily handed him one for a copper piece. He read the title:

***THE DEFINITIVE PRE-CHROMA CLASH SCOUTING REPORT***
*by Titus Heathersbee*

Caspian shrugged and started to read as he walked back to the dorm. He immediately flipped to himself and his friends to check their sections first.

**Caspian Veilbright**

*Strengths*
- Tough as nails, the man can take a beating and just keep dueling
- Cool and collected under pressure
- Immaculate fundamentals, can use simple spells to achieve big victories
*Weaknesses*
- Lack of endurance, never had a duel run long. Can he keep up the pressure?
- No trump card, not gonna see him throw a fireball to finish someone off
- Undefeated record marred by a preseason friendly loss to fellow competitor Redd Ravenshire
*Wildcard*
- Sources are reporting that the Saintillian has been hard at work honing a unique dueling style for the tournament!
**OVERALL GRADE**: B+
Summary: Caspian is a talented duelist and has the potential to go far but needs to overcome his obvious weaknesses to make a run at first.

**Auri Ashfall**

*Strengths*
- Deep spellbook, wields a wide range of offensive, defensive, and utility spells
-  Understanding of arcane principles, should be able to read her opponents intents with great accuracy
- Flexibility, can change her preparations for each opponent to tailor her strategy for optimal power
*Weaknesses*
- Inexperience, she’s risen quickly through the ranks but hasn’t performed under this big of a spotlight
- Lack of Bladesong, Auri’s signature skill isn’t allowed in spell duels, this is a significant disadvantage 
- Lack of durability, the tiefling is mighty but can she take a hit?
*Wildcard*
- Preparation, Auri has never once been unprepared to win. She will learn everything she can about her opponent to exploit to her advantage
**OVERALL GRADE**: A-
Summary: Auri is a great duelist on paper but she needs to perform on the day to make it far. Her lack of experience could be her Achilles heel. 

Redd Ravenshire

*Strengths*
- Explosive detonations, when spells collide Redd always comes out on top
- Survived a champion of the High Courts in an exhibition duel
- Resistant to mental magic, his wisdom and willpower can overcome magic that may fool or manipulate others
*Weaknesses*
- Distractions, insider sources have been saying that Redd is having personal concerns distracting him
- Predictable patterns, relies on signature spell flame blade in most duels
- Lack of physical fortitude, could use more practice dodging attacks
*Wildcard*
- Relationship with Zurr’ka Flametail, could he have been coached by one of the most talented duelists at Spark Oath?
**OVERALL GRADE**: A
Summary: A talented duelist on the field who’s battled tougher opponents than most. Not being incinerated by Klath Flametail is a feat no one else in the tourney can boast. But off the field distractions could be the end of his run. 

Sol Aelia

*Strengths*
- Illusion and Enchantment focus, doesn’t rely on a typical aggressive strategy
- Impressive endurance, has won several duels by outlasting his opponent
- Creative thinker, always thinking outside the box to get victories
*Weaknesses*
- Easily flustered, under pressure can make questionable judgement calls
- Limited offensive options, specialized in utility leaves little for outright attack
- Lacks a traditional dueling approach
*Wildcard*
- Team strength, Adrien and Kael are both formidable duelists who practice a similar style, they surely have identified their weaknesses and have prepared accordingly
**OVERALL GRADE**: B+
Summary: Sol is a creative duelist but a late addition to the clash. His style still isn’t entirely developed leading to a mismatch into some others. But strong teammates should have helped his preparation.


<sub>Feb 26, 2026 &middot; 07:55 PM UTC</sub>

Vornakir Vanori

*Strengths*
- Firepower, a draconic sorcerer with born with unmatched raw arcane power
- Confidence, this drow has never once thought he might lose a duel
- Well rounded spell preparation
*Weaknesses*
- Judgement, was banned for some time for losing his cool and using an illegal spell on an opponent
- Lack of durability, packs a punch but can’t always take one in return
- Banned, only returned to dueling a few weeks ago hard to judge his growth
*Wildcard*
- Killer Instinct, wants to win so badly he’ll do anything to get an edge over his opponent
*OVERALL GRADE**: B
Summary: Some would argue that Vornakir is an A- candidate at the least but simply we have too little information. He could be the strongest participant in the clash but his recent ban has left big questions about his ability to compete at a high level and safely.
