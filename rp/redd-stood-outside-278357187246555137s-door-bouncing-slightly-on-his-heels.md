### &#x270D; Redd

Redd stood outside @user's door, bouncing slightly on his heels like he might flee at any moment. With Zašia and Vornakir still out for the evening, he’d figured it was the perfect time to get everything ready. Now, with jam on his hands and his other roomates almost home, panic is starting to set in. From the kitchen behind him came the soft hoot of Whimsy, still rooting through the wreckage. His knuckles hovered near the door. Then he knocked—quick and light.

"Don’t worry," he called, voice pitched somewhere between flirty and 'please don’t laugh at me'. "Not here for a hookup, promise."

A pause.

"… Well. Not tonight at least."

He exhaled sharply and ran a hand through his hair, smearing a tiny bit of something—jam? Definitely jam—across his temple. His charm flickered, just for a second, revealing nerves underneath.

"I, uh… I’ve got a situation," he said finally, quieter this time. "Kind of a first. I’ve got this—this date. With Zurr’ka. Tomorrow."

The words hung there for a beat, like he couldn’t believe he’d said them out loud.

"It's not like—look, it’s not just some fling. He asked me to make something. Like, with my hands, not just my face." Redd laughed, but it was more breath than sound.

"Anyway, I tried. And, um." He gestured vaguely behind him, as if that might explain everything.

There was a loud clatter from the kitchen. A crash. A hoot. Whimsy emerged from the hallway with a piece of salami skewered on one talon like a trophy.

". . . It didn’t go great."

He looked back at the door, sheepish now.

"So. Caspian. I see you eat a lot of food. Can you please help me fix . . . whatever this is? Zurr’ka may be a Dragonborn, but I doubt even he'd eat this."
