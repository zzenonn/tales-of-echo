Vornakir

It took a little longer than anticipated. They never really established a time they wanted to experience their next date, just that it would involve lazing about the stars in a less stressful environment. Considering what they'd had to go through in the past, the Sparkian Protector assembly, and the meeting with the Archmage in their rearview, it was a good a time as any to finally take their time away from the group and revel beneath the stars.

Several attempts over the last few star filled nights, Vornakir tried to find just the perfect place and finally settled on this one. He'd taken the time to speak to some of the Professors to get special permission for access to one specific rooftop that was unparalleled for its view under the pretense that he and Auri planned to do some star charts for astrological theory independent studies. Which wasn't exactly a lie, considering during his set up for their date he'd assigned his mage hand to quickly scrawl down some journal entries and sketches of the stars above in case he was questioned.

This particular rooftop was closest to the Greenhouses where those who studied flora and fauna held classes if the Quasi was already reserved or in this case destroyed for practical use whether for academics or for the infirmary needing medical supplies. Here, there were no mage lights blinding the streets or dormlights sullying the view. Just a perfect expanse of the world above, the ever expanding cosmos and infinite stars painted across the sky.

A blanket was set out for them, and a few of his famous powdered cherry tarts were tucked away in a small tin for them to enjoy. With the assistance of Kael, someone he'd gotten closer to over the last few weeks exceptionally so, he would send for Auri to meet him at last via a message cantrip sent toward the waiting Kael. Of course, she was compensated by Vornakir agreeing to help her with a personal project of her own later. He would've sent for her himself, but this spot was in high demand by many couples for its privacy and its view. He feared leaving would free it up! The worst case scenario was fetching Auri and coming back to find Zurr'ka and Redd making up and out, and having to find a new spot.

In the meantime, he waited eagerly whilst reading the book she suggested for him - He'd bookmarked about a third of the way through already.
